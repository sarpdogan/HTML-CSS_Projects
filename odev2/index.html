MENEMEN

You'll love this easy menemen recipe of soft, perfectly scrambled eggs with tomatoes, peppers, and onions!

This simple one-pan dish comes together in 20 minutes or less, and makes the perfect breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

Just add your favorite crusty bread to serve along!

Ingredients

  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs

How to make it!

  1. Chop up one green pepper and onion,heat up some good olive oil and saut√© the onions and peppers
  2. Add 2 chopped tomatoes and 3 tablespoon of tomato paste.Cook for about 7 minutes
  3. Keep the tomato mixture in the skillet, but push it to one side. Lower the heat to medium-low, add 4 beaten eggs and cook gently until the eggs are just set.
  4. When the eggs are just set, use a wooden spoon and fold the tomato mixture into the eggs and mix gently
